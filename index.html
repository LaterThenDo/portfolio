Personal Introduction

Hi! I'm Nirob Miazee, a student currently pursuing a diploma in Game Development & Technology at Nanyang Polytechnic. My journey into game development began out of pure curiosity when I was nine. With some free time on my hands, I started playing with Roblox and eventually stumbled upon Roblox Studio. That early boredom turned into a passion for developing games which has never changed ever since. I enjoy working on action and stealth-based games, getting inspiration from big titles like Call of Duty, Ready or Not, Hitman, and Assassin's Creed. While most of my personal projects are solo, I actually prefer working in a team as collaborating with others helps me stay motivated, explore new perspectives, make it easier to solve problems and divide the workload.
