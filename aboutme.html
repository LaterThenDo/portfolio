About Me

Hi, I'm Nirob Miazee. I am currently pursuing a diploma in Game Development & Technology at Nanyang Polytechnic. I have always been fascinated by games since I was nine, which was when I stumbled across Roblox Studio out of boredom. That curiosity quickly grew into a long-standing interest in creating games.

My passion lies mainly in programming, especially action and stealth-based games. Games such as Hitman, Ready or Not, Call of Duty, and Assassin's Creed have all influenced the types of games I would like to develop. I am still exploring ideas, but my focus always comes back to gameplay and mechanics over storytelling.

Outside of game development, I stay active by practicing judo in school, while occasionally playing badminton. When I have free time, I like experiment with side projects in Roblox Studio, though I tend to move on quickly once the initial excitement of making the game fades. I'm always looking to sharpen my skills and push myself with new programming challenges. You can read more on my hobbies here.

Core Values

Accountability

I work best when there is a clear deadline in place, something or someone to answer to. In solo projects, it is easy for me to lose the initial excitement without external pressure, but when I have responsibilities or expectations to meet, I stay focused and driven. I treat every task like it matters, and that mindset helps me push projects across the finish line.

Collaboration

I believe that a good team works best when everyone feels confident in their role. I try my best to step into roles where I feel most capable, but I also try to make sure everyone else is in the right place too. Whether that means adjusting responsibilities or supporting others, I value collaboration as a way to motivate each other and create something better together.

Experience

At the core of every project I work on is the player's experience. I care most about how a game feels, how immersive or enjoyable it is. Coding and features matter, but they're only tools to serve that experience. I always ask myself how things could be better for the player, and that constant focus helps me improve with every version I build.

Future Goals

I know that I want to keep exploring the technical side of game development, especially in areas like coding and AI systems. I am particularly drawn to FPS and stealth-based action games, and one of my long-term hopes is to contribute to a project with the level of polish and impact of something like Call of Duty.

Right now, I am focused on building my skills, working on personal projects, and learning what makes gameplay feel responsive, smart, and immersive. Whether I eventually join a game studio or work with a small team of developers, I am excited to keep growing through collaboration and problem-solving. The goal is not just to finish games, but to keep getting better with each one.
